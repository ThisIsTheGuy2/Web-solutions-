Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers to help you make the right decision.

How long does it take to build the website?
Typically, a Basic package takes about 1-2 weeks from the moment I have all your content (text and images). Professional and Premium packages usually take 2-4 weeks to ensure everything is perfectly polished and tested.
Do I need to write my own content?
Yes, you know your business best! You will need to provide the text (services, about us, etc.) and any specific photos you want to use. If you need help with copywriting, let me know and I can recommend local professionals.
What are the ongoing costs?
Beyond the initial build price, you will need to pay for your domain name (usually around R150-R300/year) and basic hosting. If you opt for my Maintenance Plan (R750/month), I handle updates, form reliability, and basic hosting for you.
Do you use WordPress?
No. I custom code my websites using modern technologies. This means your site will be significantly faster, more secure (no plugins to update or get hacked), and won't be bloated with unnecessary code.
